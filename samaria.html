Intro / Challenge

As a passionate people lover, I cherish building meaningful relationships. And one of the ways I do that is by being intentional about the experiences and memories that I am I am attached to in the lives of my loved ones.Â I had always wished there was a product to help me remember and celebrate their special days. But beyond just the normal wishes everybody else does, I also would love to be able to send them gifts, easily, irrespective of how far away they are from me. Then I thought, why not go ahead and design it? So the focus of this project was to design the app.

  • Solution: A mobile app that helps you remember, congratulate/celebrate and send gifts to your loved ones on their special days.

Some Features

    The main features will be:
  • Get notified of special days that are saved on your calendar or posted by "connections" (you're able to do the same too).
  • Send and receive wishes and gifts.
  • Post a wish list and get contributions for it or get it paid for by your friends and loved ones.
