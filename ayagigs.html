Intro / Challenge

African freelancers lack adequate access to the global marketplace because the existing options are limited and restrictive especially when it comes to payment.

statement: "How might we connect African freelancers to the global gig marketplace and also empower them to manage those projects properly to ensure quality delivery and get paid without hiccups."



  • Role: User Interface Designer
  • Tools: Figma + Pen & Paper.
  • Outcome:UX & UI Design, Design System, UI Design, & Interactive Prototypes.

Research & Insights

The Solution

Ayagigs: A global talent marketplace uniquely tailored for African freelancers powered by Cryptocurrency.

Value Proposition

"For skilled Africans freelancers aged 18 - 45 who are familiar with cryptocurrency that needs access to global jobs and borderless payments, our product Ayagigs is a Freelancer Platform that helps them get jobs, manage their projects, and get paid easily with a P2P digital payment system."

Project Goals

Freelancers: A web app that helps freelancers quickly view and easily apply for freelance gigs.

Businesses: A web app that helps businesses find vetted freelancers for their business needs.
